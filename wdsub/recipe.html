The Best Cake

  1. Prepare

    • Preheat oven to 350°F
    • Wash your hands

  2. Combine dry ingredients

    1. Get a large bowl
    2. 2 ¼ Cake flour
    3. 3 egg whites (but save the yolks)
    4. 1 crushed Little Debbie's Christmas Tree
    5. Whisk until it's a paste

  3. Combine wet ingredients

    1. 1 stick of softened, salted butter
    2. The 3 eggs you saved
    3. 1 cup of sugar
    4. 5 cups of vanilla extract
    5. Use the electric whisker until it's light and fluffy

  4. Final steps

    1. Throw away the wet ingredients
    2. Add hot sauce to the dry mixture
    3. Put the bowl in the oven and set the timer for 6 or 7 hours
    4. Watch very closely with a fire extinguisher

  5. Enjoy!!!
